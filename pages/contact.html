Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common B2B inquiries

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

+

Our MOQ varies by product type: School backpacks (500 pieces), Sports backpacks (300 pieces), Travel backpacks (200 pieces), Laptop backpacks (400 pieces), and Outdoor backpacks (250 pieces). We can discuss flexible arrangements for long-term partnerships.

How long does production take?

+

Standard production time is 15-25 days after sample approval and deposit payment. Rush orders can be accommodated in 10-15 days with additional fees. Complex customizations may require 25-35 days.

Do you provide samples before production?

+

Yes, we provide samples for approval before mass production. Sample cost is $50-150 per piece depending on complexity, which is refundable upon order confirmation. Sample production takes 5-7 days.

What customization options are available?

+

We offer comprehensive customization including logo printing (embroidery, screen printing, heat transfer), color combinations, size variations, material selection, zipper colors, hardware finishes, and interior layouts.

What are your payment terms?

+

Standard payment terms: 30% deposit upon order confirmation, 70% balance before shipment. We accept T/T, L/C, and PayPal. For established clients, we offer flexible payment arrangements.

Do you handle international shipping?

+

Yes, we ship worldwide with established logistics partners. We handle all export documentation and can arrange FOB, CIF, or DDP terms. Shipping typically takes 7-15 days depending on destination.
